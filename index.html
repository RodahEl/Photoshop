Photoshop

Photoshop is a software used by designers, web professionals, video editors, and photographers to alter or manipulate digital images. I chose to learn photoshop since My career leans towards web design and developement

About this course

This course Involves in-depth technical investigation of digital imaging on the computer using image editing and/or image creation software. Manipulation, creation, and editing of digital images for a wide assortment of output. In this course we used the Adobe Photoshop CC Classroom in a Book Textbook and, the Adobe Photoshop CC software. The text editor I used throughtout the course when needed was notepad++.

Selected Labs

For this project, I selected the top 6 labs that got good feedback after being graded. Some of the images were posters and did not require a before and after Comparison. In my Labs, I used either images taken with my camera or images that I downloaded from public domain websites that offered free images for personal and commercial use. In most of my Labs, I chose to edit/manipulate photos involving nature, it's easy for users and viewers to relate to and, I love nature.

Correcting Color Tint or Changing color hue

This image is from Lab 4 part C. I took this photo using my phone in downtown Dallas. I cropped the image to remove a traffic light, I enhanced the appearance of the Gold statue using hue & saturation. I also touched a little bit on the contrast and brightness of the image

Magazine Cover Photo

The task in this lab was to find a photo that I liked and use it to create a magazine cover. I found a photo from a public domain website. I Created a Travel Magazine cover, added 2 Text layers(magazine name & 100 travel ideas), I added a gradient to the Travel layer and, added 2 layers to 100 ideas(inner shadow, inner glow, and stroke).

Layers and Layer Blends

This image is from Lab 3 part B where I had to work with two images. I created two layers, both with images(Forest and Leopard). I used the lighten feature as a blend option and No opacity was used. I also used the lasso tool to soften the edges of the leopard image since the distinction was too obvious that there were two photos. I adjusted the contrast and brightness features just a little. I also resized the 2nd image to make it smaller than the background layer. Both images were imported from a public domain website.

Combining Images

This image is from Lab 4 Part A whare I had to combine two images. I used Mask on one of the layers, Used some refine edge to smooth it out, and, the brush tool to make it as smooth as possible. Both images were resized from the original size

Masks and Corrections

This image is from Lab part B. I resized the image from the original size. I adjusted the appearance using levels, curves, hue & saturation, and, shadows

Photoshop Quick Fixes

Used Content Aware Patch to fill the top left corner and top right corner of the photo. I cropped out the bottom of the photo to remove part of the wooden wall the birds are sitting on. I adjusted the exposure, brightness and situration since the original photo that I took using a phone was not too clear.
